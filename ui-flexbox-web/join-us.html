An Opportunity for Students to Shape the Future

The UESEC is actively seeking talented students and graduates in fields such as:

  • Aerospace Engineering
  • Astrophysics and Planetary Science
  • Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence
  • Medicine and Space Biology
  • Environmental Science and Sustainability
  • Communication, Outreach, and Policy Development


These roles are not just about solving technical challenges but also about shaping the future of humanity. As part of UESEC, students and young professionals will work on projects such as:

  • Establishing permanent colonies on Mars.
  • Exploring the potential for life on Europa.
  • Developing cutting-edge technologies for interstellar travel and planetary sustainability.
  • Pioneering new scientific discoveries that will redefine our understanding of the universe.

Empowering Career Centers to Inspire Students

We are inviting all colleges and universities to join this exciting initiative by becoming UESEC Outreach Partners. By working with campus career centers, we aim to inform, inspire, and recruit the next generation of space explorers.

Career centers will play a critical role in:

  1. Hosting Recruitment Events: UESEC representatives will visit campuses to share insights about our missions and career opportunities.
  2. Promoting Job Opportunities: Career centers can advertise internships, co-op positions, and full-time roles with UESEC.
  3. Facilitating Application Support: Workshops and resources will help students prepare competitive applications.

Call to Action for Colleges and Universities

We encourage career centers to:

  • Promote UESEC opportunities: Share information through newsletters, job boards, and social media.
  • Host information sessions: Partner with UESEC representatives to organize events on campus.
  • Highlight interdisciplinary opportunities: Encourage students from all fields of study to consider how their skills can contribute to space exploration.

How to Get Involved

Career centers interested in partnering with UESEC can contact us directly at UESEC-Outreach@nasa.gov to access promotional materials, schedule recruitment events, and receive exclusive updates about upcoming missions and programs.
