Explore the Cosmos with UESEC

Join us in shaping humanity’s future beyond Earth.

The United Earth Space Exploration Command (UESEC) is the culmination of humanity’s long-standing dream to explore and colonize the stars. Its origins trace back to secretive military space programs in the mid-20th century, evolving through decades of scientific discovery, geopolitical collaboration, and technological breakthroughs. Below is an overview of how UESEC came to be.

NASA Partners with Global Space Agencies to Form the United Earth Space Exploration Command (UESEC) NASA, in collaboration with international space agencies and academic institutions, is proud to announce the establishment of the United Earth Space Exploration Command (UESEC). This historic coalition is dedicated to advancing humanity’s journey into space by fostering global cooperation, innovation, and exploration. The UESEC aims to recruit the brightest minds from around the world to lead groundbreaking missions, from establishing sustainable colonies on Mars to unlocking the secrets of Europa’s oceans and beyond. To achieve this ambitious vision, NASA is partnering with colleges and universities across the United States and Canada to encourage students to explore career opportunities with UESEC through their campus career centers.
